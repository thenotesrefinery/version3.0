Notes Upload Guidelines and Rules

 

Content you can upload:

  • You can share notes and essays for any subject examinable in the Singapore GCSE O Level and A Level syllabus.
  • Some notes you can share include: Summary notes, formula sheet, exam question answering tips, mindmaps, essay writing skills, essay outlines, case studies and examples list.
  • Essays (and essay outlines): If you are sharing essays, please send the full essay/outline and include a rough grade received for your work.
  • Self-made notes are highly encouraged. You can refer to different resources and online content but you should make references and edits.
  • Please do not share books or your school notes!
  • Uploads that include inappropriate contents will not be accepted (refer to Terms of Use for more information).

 

Before you submit or share any content, please adhere to these rules:

  1. Respect Copyrights. In the notes you are uploading, please ensure that they are original and do not have content that is copied wholesale from others. If you have copied/used content from others, please make sure that you have obtained the permission of the original creators themselves.
  2. Do not upload any offensive, obscene, misleading, or questionable material. You are responsible for the content you share, so please be considerate and make notes sharing a fun process!

 

Content license and protection of content shared on thenotesrefinery:

Any notes or information you upload or share on thenotesrefinery will be shared on thenotesrefinery or any subsidiary of thenotesrefinery  following the Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International License under Creative Commons (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0).  

This means that:

  1. The notes and content you share can be shared and adapted by other users
  2. Attribution: Members have to give you credit and indicate if changes are made to your original content.
  3. Non-commercial: All notes and content should never be used for commercial purposes. This is true for thenotesrefinery (TNR) and for all members using our platform. Notes and content shared on TNR are to be open-sourced.
  4. ShareAlike: If any member is to remix, transform, or build upon the content shared, he/she must distribute the contributions under the same license as the original.

After your notes are uploaded, we will verify and process them. If they are approved, they will be watermarked with the Creative Commons watermark along with your username. They will then be posted onto thenotesrefinery and can only be accessed by other members who have contributed their notes.
